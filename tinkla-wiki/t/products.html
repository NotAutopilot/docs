Our Products

Tinkla Buddy

Let the magic begin! Do you have a Tesla Model S with MCU1? Tinkla Buddy is the Swiss Army knife for MCU1. From integrating OpenPilot into your Instrument Cluster to helping you troubleshoot issues with your car, there is nothing like it.
Tinkla Relay

Do you like adding extra accesories to your Tesla but you don't want to add knobs and switches to control them? Tinkla Relay is the answer. It used combinations of steering wheel buttons and/or events to trigger up to 4 relays.
Tinkla OBD-C Adapter

The easiest way to connect your Comma2 or Comma3 to your preAP Tesla Model S. It has also connectors for radar and pedal interceptor, if you choose to install the full array of options.
Custom Software Development

Are you working on an EV conversion? Do you need to modify CAN messages on the fly or to make components from different cars to talk to eachother? We specialize in custom software development to ensure your project is a success.
Consulting

Do you need a little (or more) help with your EV or ADAS project? Give us a call. We have experience with many EVs and EV technologies as well as ADAS and Machine Learning.
