## Limitations & Known Issues

### System Limitations

**NotAutopilot is limited by:**

1. **Braking Capability**
   - Regenerative braking only (no hydraulic brake control)
   - NOT sufficient for emergency stops
   - Limited effectiveness at low speeds
   - Reduced capability in cold weather or low battery

2. **Environmental Factors**
   - Camera requires clear windshield
   - Performance degraded in rain, snow, fog
   - Radar can be affected by weather and dirt
   - Low light affects camera recognition

3. **Road Conditions**
   - Requires clear lane markings
   - Designed for paved roads only
   - May not handle complex intersections
   - Not suitable for all driving scenarios

4. **Detection Limitations**
   - May not detect all obstacles or vehicles
   - False positives and false negatives possible
   - Limited field of view
   - Occlusion and shadows affect detection

5. **Control Limitations**
   - Lane keeping requires good lane markings
   - ACC limited by regenerative braking
   - Speed control has minimum speed limits
   - System may disengage unexpectedly

### Known Issues

- System performance varies with conditions
- Occasional false warnings possible
- Camera calibration may require adjustment
- Radar calibration critical for performance
- Software updates may introduce new issues
- Hardware failures possible
- Integration issues with some vehicle variants

**These are fundamental limitations, not software bugs.**
