# Open Pilot for Tesla Model S/X with AP2

## Quick Start

1. Buy an [AP2 Harness](AP2_Harness.md) from our online store
2. [Install the Comma three](Comma_devkit.md) in your windshield.
3. Connect your [Comma Three](https://comma.ai/shop/products/three) to the [AP2 Harness](AP2_Harness.md) using long USB-C cable.
4. Connect your [Comma Three](https://comma.ai/shop/products/three) to the Black Panda and then to the second port of the [AP2 Harness](AP2_Harness.md) using long USB-C cable.
5. [Comma_devkit#Installing_Open Pilot_for_Tesla|Install the Tesla version of OP]]
6. Finally take your Tesla w/OP for a spin!
